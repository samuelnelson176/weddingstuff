CHAIRMAN'S ADDRESS

A moment of welcome, blessing, and honour.

Mr. Oluwarotimi Akinrinade Adewole MD/CEO Samvic Insurance Brokers

Mr. Oluwarotimi Akinrinade Adewole is a distinguished Nigerian business executive with a proven track record in Insurance... Mr. Oluwarotimi Akinrinade Adewole is a distinguished Nigerian business executive with a proven track record in Insurance and Risk Management. As Managing Director & CEO of Sam-Vic Insurance Brokers Limited, he spearheads strategic growth and innovation in the company. A highly qualified professional, Oluwarotimi holds an HND in Insurance from the Polytechnic of Ibadan (1995), an MBA from the University of Abuja (2008), and an MSc in Insurance and Risk Management from BAYES Business School, London (2011). He has also attended several leadership and professional courses within and outside the country which includes training at Willis Tower Watson (WTW) London, Price Forbes London, AON Insurance Brokers London. He is an Associate of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Nigeria (AIIN), Associate Chartered Institute of Management (ANIM), Associate Chartered Insurance Institute of London (ACII), Fellow of the Nigerian Council of Registered Insurance Brokers (FCIB), and Fellow of the Chartered National Institute of Marketing of Nigeria (FNIMN). With over 28 years of industry experience, Oluwarotimi has held key roles, including Senior Manager at Boff & Company Insurance Brokers Limited where he trained as an Insurance Broker for Thirteen (13) years before promoting Sam-Vic Insurance Brokers Limited in 2011. He is a committed member of Junior Chamber International (JCI), a worldwide leadership organisation serving as President of JCI Aso (2006), National Convention Director (2009), Director Project & Programs (2010), Vice Chairman of JCI Nigeria Senate Association (2020-2022) and Chairman of JCI Nigeria Senate Association (2022-2024). A Chartered Insurance Practitioner (CII, London) and a committed Governing Board member of Nigerian Council of Registered Insurance Brokers (NCRIB) currently serving as the Vice President of the council. Oluwarotimi is married and blessed with two beautiful daughters.
