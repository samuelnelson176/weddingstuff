Our Love Story

What began as a simple interaction over fifteen years ago gradually unfolded into meaningful conversations and shared perspectives. Over time, our values aligned, trust deepened, and friendship became the solid foundation upon which love quietly grew.

We never imagined that we would one day stand as husband and wife. We were simply committed to friendship, genuine, unforced, and steady. Yet love has a way of emerging from the most unexpected places. Even now, we remain in awe of what God has done.

From our first intentional outing, it became clear that what we shared was not fleeting. Our conversations flowed effortlessly, from law and leadership to faith, nation-building, and the kind of family we hoped to build someday.

Nothing about our journey was rushed. It was built. Carefully. Prayerfully. Intentionally.

The proposal reflected exactly who we are, intimate and deeply personal. There were no theatrics. Just clarity. A question asked with certainty. A “Yes” given with peace.

Today, we step into forever with a shared commitment: - To build not just a marriage, but a legacy. - To create a home marked by warmth, wisdom, and generational impact. - To love deliberately, faithfully, and wholeheartedly.
