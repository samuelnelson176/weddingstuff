Our Love Story

Some connections feel familiar from the very beginning, and ours was one of them.

When we met, there was an ease between us, an unforced alignment of values, ambition, humour, and depth in the realm of the supernatural because the deep calls forth the deep. What started as conversation soon became companionship. What began as friendship gradually revealed something greater.

We were drawn to each other’s clarity of purpose and calm confidence. We admired one another’s strength of character, leadership instincts, and steady devotion to growth. Together, we discovered something rare, two individuals equally committed to building, learning, and evolving.

Our dates were never ordinary. They were prayerful conversations about vision. About faith. About investments. About family structure. About impact. We did not simply fall in love; we grew into it.

Through life’s demands, professional commitments, and seasons of personal development, we chose partnership daily. We challenged one another. Supported one another. Prayed together. Planned together.

The proposal was between both of us alone, thoughtful and symbolic, reflecting not just romance, but readiness. When the question was asked, it was not simply about marriage; it was about building together. And when the answer was given, it was not just to a wedding, but to a shared destiny.

Our union represents alignment, spiritually, intellectually, and purposefully.

We do not have the liberty to choose who our parents are, however, it is a duty to our children unborn, to chose the best Father and Mother for them.

We are not just getting married. We are building a future.
